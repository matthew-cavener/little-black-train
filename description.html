Little Black Train

5 stranger find themselves on a little black train. Might as well chat, there's nothing else to do! Very work-in-progress

Controls

  • Arrow keys to move
  • Space/Enter to interact
  • Arrow keys or mouse to select dialogue options

Credits

Inspired by the folk music of Woody Guthrie, Steve Earle, and Tyler Childers (among others!)
